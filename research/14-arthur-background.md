## AHFE 2024 Publication

**Title**: "A Novel Agent-Based Framework for Conversational Data Analysis and Personal AI Systems"

- **Authors**: Kurylek, Bartosz; Camara, Arthur; Nandi, Akash; Markopoulos, Evangelos
- **Published in**: Artificial Intelligence and Social Computing, pp. 124--136 (2024)
- **Conference**: AHFE (Applied Human Factors and Ergonomics) International Conference 2024
- **DOI**: 10.54941/ahfe1004649
- **UCL Discovery**: https://discovery.ucl.ac.uk/id/eprint/10195658/
- **Open Access**: https://openaccess.cms-conferences.org/publications/book/978-1-958651-98-8/article/978-1-958651-98-8_13

**Abstract summary**: The paper introduces an agent-based framework that leverages conversational data to enhance LLMs with personalized knowledge, enabling "Artificial Personal Intelligence" (API) systems. The framework addresses collecting and analysing unstructured conversational data using LLM agents and embeddings to process, organize, and extract insights from conversations. The system integrates knowledge data aggregation (multi-level hierarchy for organizing information by conceptual similarity and topical relevance) with agent-based conversational data extraction. Effectiveness demonstrated through empirical analysis of real-world conversational data and user survey.

**Skills demonstrated**: LLM Agents, Conversation Analysis, Visualization, Prompt Engineering, Python, Vector Embeddings, Scientific Paper Writing.

**Prototype**: https://insights-ai.web.app

**Supervisor**: Evangelos Markopoulos (UCL School of Management / Queen Mary University of London)
