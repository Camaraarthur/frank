## Networks and Multiplier Channels

These are not individual outreach targets but platforms/networks where Frank can reach many councillors at once.

### The Community Councillor Collective
- **Website:** thecommunitycouncillorcollective.co.uk
- **What:** Cross-party network of councillors committed to participatory democracy. Founded by Fiona Barrows (Frome). Supported by Involve, the Democracy Network, and the Iswe Foundation.
- **Action:** Contact Fiona Barrows. Offer to present Frank at their next event or on their online platform.

### LGA Young Councillors Weekender
- **Website:** local.gov.uk (councillor development section)
- **What:** Annual event for councillors under 40, held every November. 15th year running. Cross-party.
- **Action:** Apply to present or exhibit at the November 2026 event. Young councillors are the exact demographic for Frank.

### LGA Digital Inclusion Network
- **What:** Meets every two months. Councils share best practice on digital inclusion.
- **Action:** Get Frank on the agenda as a case study in digital civic engagement.

### LGA Transformation Network
- **What:** Chaired by Debbie Knopp (Essex CC). Meets every two months. Discusses digital transformation in councils.
- **Action:** Present Frank as a zero-cost transformation tool for resident engagement.

### mySociety / TICTeC
- **Website:** mysociety.org | tictec.mysociety.org
- **What:** Leading UK civic tech organisation. Runs TICTeC conference (212 participants from 37 countries in 2025). Offers GBP5,000 grants for council-civic tech partnerships. Currently exploring European Civic Tech Hub with Sitra.
- **Action:** Apply for mySociety grant to pilot Frank with a council partner. Submit to TICTeC 2026 conference.

### Civic Future
- **Website:** civicfuture.org
- **What:** Network focused on civic innovation.
- **Action:** Get listed and connected.

### Green Party Councillors Network
- **What:** The Green Party now has 859+ councillors on 181 councils -- an all-time high. Many are recent defectors from Labour who are actively looking for new ways of working.
- **Action:** Contact London Green Party (london.greenparty.org.uk) to present Frank at a councillor training or networking event.

### Flatpack Democracy Network
- **What:** ~20 councils run on participatory/independent models inspired by Frome. These are councils philosophically aligned with Frank.
- **Action:** Route through Fiona Barrows / Community Councillor Collective.
